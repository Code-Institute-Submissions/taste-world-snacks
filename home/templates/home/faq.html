The basics

Tast world snacks is a subscription service for those who love trying snacks from around the world. Each month we deliver a box of snacks to your door from a different country. You can choose to pick your own box every month or choose to get surprised every month.
It’s pretty simple, just just go to get a box on our site, then select what kind box you want. Lastly what kind of payment plan suits you and provide all the necessary details. We will then take over the process of feeding you the best tasting snacks from around the world.
You can expect to receive snacks from sweets to crisps to biscuits to chocolates and lot more. We include all types of snacks and only pack the best and most delicious snacks from around the world! You can get an idea of the snacks we send by checking out our country page or our shop.
Currently we do two type of boxes, the Normal Taste world snacks box and the Jumbo Tast world snacks box. The Normal box contains 8-10+ full size snacks, whilst the Jumbo box contains 16-20+ full size snacks.

Shipping information

Currently we ship internationally to the following countries: United Kingdom, Ireland, Netherlands, Canada, France, Italy & the United States of America – additional shipping charges will apply for these addresses. International shipping cost can be calculated at checkout.

If you do not see your country here, please contact us to see if we can make an exception.
Your first box will be shipped to you soon after you sign up (usually within 5-7 business days). For existing customers your account will renew on the 4th day of the month (this is when payments are taken out) and your box will be shipped out on the 17th of every month.

You can always check the status of your order (and see when it will ship) in your account settings.
International delivery dates can vary, but we try to get them to you as fast as possible!

You can track your delivery using your order number. For more information please contact us.
To change your shipping address it’s pretty simple.

1. First login to your account. 2. Click on “Addresses tab” on the left hand side of the screen. 3. Then “edit” button next to where it says shipping address. 4. Change your shipping address in the boxes. 5. Press save address when you’re done.

Please note, you need to change your address approximately a week before the next shipping date (i.e. a week before the 17th), or you risk your box being delivered to the old address!

Billing information

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover and American Express credit cards and debit cards from our Checkout page. All of our payments are processed through the Stripe Payment Processor.
You will be billed for your first month immediately upon signup. Your plan will automatically renew (don’t worry, you can cancel anytime, from your account settings) on the 4th of the month.
Coupon codes are only applied to your first box, and every box after that will be charged regular price. Follow us on our social media and sign up to our newsletter so you don’t miss out on coupon codes!
